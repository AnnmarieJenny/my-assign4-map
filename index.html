Vacant Lots in
New York City

This map displays two sets of vacant lots in New York City, as listed below. Toggle between these two maps using the buttons on the upper right of the map. Click on a particular lot for more details.

All Vacant Lots is derived from NYC PLUTO and displays all lots designated as vacant by the city. This includes some parks and other open space geographies.

Development Ready Vacant Lots is derived from NYC OpenData and shows publicly-owned vacant lots more conducive to new development.

To gain further insight on each lot, use the BBL listed within a given pop-up and visit this NYC Building Lookup Page for even more details.

*These maps build off of previous work on NYC vacant lots, viewable here.
